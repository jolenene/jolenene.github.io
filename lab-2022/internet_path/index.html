Internet Path

A healing journey guided by koolkat, your internet friend.

                'KoolKat' was a character I made up randomly when I was feeling down. 
                He is blue, with a star on his chest. He is modeled after 'Doraemon', a 
                cartoon character from my childhood. The character is shown to always have
                a solution to every problem, and I want my avatar to be the same as well.

Process of Development

 
                The character was drawn when I was doodling to take my mind off of stuff that made me sad.
                I ran into some trouble having the button display three external link buttons at once, but 
                later on figured out how to use jquery to do it.

Reflections on the Project

            It was gratifying seeing the pop-up buttons work. I had wanted to make something
                 like that for so long and finally succeeded. The animating took a long time and
              was a little difficult. So was getting every div in the correct order and making
                 sure all the links worked. The gif buttons were a success. I learned how to use jquery,
                 which was very satisfying.
